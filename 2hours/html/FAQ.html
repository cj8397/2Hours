FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

 

1. What are the fees for using our service?

2. How do I cancel an order?

3. What if I received food that is not up to standard?





























Answers

1. There are two options to choose from: a) pay a one time fee for each order or b) pay a monthly subscription.



Top

2. Once an order is placed, you have up to two hours to cancel that order. If the order is cancelled after 2 hours, only the product amount will be refunded, not the service charge (if you paid a one time service fee). For our monthly subscribers, orders can be cancelled up to 2 hours before delivery time.



Top

3. In the unlikely event produce is delivered in poor condition, we will issue a coupon for your next order of equivalent value.
